Drachenwald Court Reporter

Tools & data

Awards are fetched from op.drachenwald.sca.org on demand. When “Verify names via OP search” is enabled, the SCA and mundane names you enter are sent to op.drachenwald.sca.org/search to confirm spelling. Disable the toggle to keep names off the network entirely. Drafts are stored locally on this device only.

Privacy

GDPR notice: this extension does not transmit recipient names, mundane names, or any draft text to any server. The only outbound network requests are (1) when you click "Refresh awards list" (public award list from op.drachenwald.sca.org), and (2) when you click "Insert into form", which writes directly into the form fields on the page you have open. Drafts are kept in chrome.storage.local until you clear them.
